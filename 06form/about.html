Artist of the Week is a website dedicated to the recognition of contemporary artists. It acts as a place of exploration, discussion, and inspiration for artists and art lovers everywhere. There is a new spotlight every week. Recommendations can be submitted through the contact page.

The site was created in 2025 as a school project by Kim Shepro, a student at Utah Valley University, and has continually been upkept since then.
